Aviel Goh

I am an (aspiring) Full Stack Web Developer with a keen interest in technology and how it can be used to solve interesting and complex problems. Upon graduating from The University of Melbourne with a Bachelor of Commerce (majors in Finance & Accounting), I began my career as a Tax Consultant at Ernst & Young (EY) where I worked with and advised a range of domestic and foreign clients. Whilst I enjoyed the opportunity to develop professionally in a corporate environment, once I started dabbling in code I quickly realised that my passion was to work with technology that creates the future.

To pursue this further, I left my job at EY to undertake the Web Development Immersive course at General Assembly and thoroughly enjoyed the fast-paced learning environment and the thrill of picking up new technologies quickly. More than just learning the theory, I gained practical experience in implementing the languages and frameworks taught to create functioning web applications and feel confident in my ability to continue learning new technologies.

To me, technology is a way of facilitating meaningful connections between both people and objects and I am eager to build great products that I can be proud of, whether they are of my own design or with an amazing team solving a real-world problem. If you're interested in helping me make this happen, I'd love to buy you a coffee (or ice cream) and chat further.
